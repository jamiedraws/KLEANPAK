KLEAN/pak is so easy to use.

The KLEAN/pak is an industrial grade, portable mass disinfection system that is designed to provide disinfecting solution over a large area with an easy to use standard garden hose water supply. Using the KLEAN/pak, with appropriate disinfecting solutions, gives a quick and easy way to disinfect larger items and items with areas that are difficult to reach with a wipe. Since the KLEAN/pak is not an aerosol dispenser, the spray droplets are larger and won't get carried away by the wind, resulting in more disinfecting solution making it to the items being disinfected.
